Sentio Necromancer version 1.0 Release Notes
-------------------------------------------------

This is the initial release of the Sentio Necromancer.

Sentio Characters are a new line of fully-rigged AI-driven characters that can be rapidly added to any game.  Look for additional Sentio Characters on the Unity Asset Store or at rivaltheory.com/store.
